Privacy Policy

// Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

At OnePoint, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you engage our web development and domain management services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect essential information to provide our services, including:

  • Contact Info: Name, email, phone number, and company details.
  • Service Data: Information required for domain registration and technical support.
  • Technical Data: Usage metrics collected automatically through our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to fulfill your requests: managing domain registrations, providing website maintenance, communicating project updates, and processing service payments.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share necessary contact information with authorized third-party service providers, such as domain registrars, exclusively to complete the registration services you have requested.

5. Data Security & Your Rights

We implement industry-standard security to protect your info. You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold about you at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding your data, please contact us via our Contact Portal.
