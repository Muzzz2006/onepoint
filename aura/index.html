Common Queries

FAQ

Is Aura Academy fully accredited?

Yes. Aura operates a double-matrix profile. Graduates receive an accredited Master of Fine Arts or Digital Space Design Degree alongside portfolio validation verified directly by our global gallery curators.

What does the evaluation process look like?

We ignore standard standardized academic parameters. Applicants submit a single raw layout concept, code canvas asset, or traditional illustration sketch. Selection is based purely on underlying visionary imagination.

Can I participate remotely or is it physical only?

Both. Our studio live streams and digital painting modules are explicitly configured for independent remote cohorts, while tactile layout and spatial installation workshops take place inside physical space labs.
